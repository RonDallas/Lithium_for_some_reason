 through death, through life;

        Self unseen      rings throughout
        Self of selves   rightward doubt

  then lines converge on every plane,
          Connection lost -        Connections gained,

A blade brings death—
Your life, like a blade—
Alive, and yet.


Inform your self to see clearer,
in morning dawn, three more.


Living in hell starts to seem OK.
Then I see you bring nothing.

%% LITHSKY_lost_repeat_1
The cycle goes on, uninterrupted.
   Blades seek me out,
           I cherish
         their brevity.

On instinct I dwell,
   I stay undenied.

  Repeat, repeat, repeat again —
   blades speak truths we
    can not say ourselves.
